preparing Tetrahydrofuran

  • Reaction to form Tetrahydrofuran

  • THF can be synthesized from the catalytic hydrogenation of Furan. The process of manufacturing THF in the industry uses dehydration of 1,4-butanediol with an acid catalyst. Du Pont developed the THF production process by oxidizing n-butane to rough maleic anhydride, followed by hydrogenation of maleic anhydride to THF
  • THF pretends to form peroxides if stored in opened case. Therefore, THF should not be distilled to dry, which can leave explosive peroxide residues. Commercial THF often uses BHT to prevent the formation of peroxides.
  • Silver(I) triflate catalyzes intramolecular additions of hydroxyl or carboxyl groups to olefins in good to excellent yields for a range of substrates under relatively mild conditions. This reaction is one of the simplest methods to construct cyclic ethers or lactones.Founded by N. W. Reich,in 2004
